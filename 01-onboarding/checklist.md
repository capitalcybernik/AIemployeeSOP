# Client Onboarding Checklist

## Before Discovery Call

- [ ] Client name and business type
- [ ] Website URL (if exists)
- [ ] Current pain points (what prompted interest)
- [ ] Tier interest (Standard $500 or Pro $1,000)
- [ ] Referred by (agency partner, direct, etc.)

## Discovery Call (30 min)

### Business Understanding
- [ ] What does the business do?
- [ ] How many locations/service areas?
- [ ] Current team size
- [ ] Who handles marketing today? (nobody, in house, agency)
- [ ] What CRM/tools do they use? (Tekmetric, Steer, ShopWare, etc.)
- [ ] Current online presence (Google reviews count, website quality, social media activity)

### AI Employee Scope
- [ ] Which skills matter most? (reference skills catalog)
- [ ] Which platform(s) should it deploy on? (website chat, SMS, email, Discord)
- [ ] What should it NEVER do? (discuss pricing, make promises, share internal info)
- [ ] Who is the escalation contact? (name, phone, email)
- [ ] Business hours and after hours behavior
- [ ] Brand voice and tone (formal, casual, friendly, professional)

### Technical Details
- [ ] Domain/website access (if deploying live chat)
- [ ] Email access (if deploying email skills)
- [ ] Social media accounts (if deploying social skills)
- [ ] Any existing automations or tools to be aware of

## Post Discovery Call

- [ ] Document all requirements in client folder
- [ ] Assign tier and confirm skills selection
- [ ] Send welcome email with timeline (2 week target)
- [ ] Create client entry in tracking sheet
- [ ] Begin build process (see build guides)
