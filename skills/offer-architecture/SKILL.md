# Offer Architecture - Complete Service & Product Structure

**Name:** Offer Architecture  
**Description:** Documents the client's complete service and product structure including tiers, pricing, features, ideal customers, upsell paths, and bundling strategies for accurate sales content and proposal creation.

## What This Skill Does

The Offer Architecture skill creates a comprehensive map of all services, products, and pricing structures. This includes service tiers, feature comparisons, ideal customer profiles for each offering, upsell and cross-sell opportunities, bundling strategies, and pricing psychology. The AI Employee uses this information to create accurate sales content, proposals, and to answer pricing questions appropriately based on the prospect's needs and budget level.

This architecture ensures the AI Employee never undersells, oversells, or misrepresents what's available, and can guide prospects to the right solution at the right price point.

## How to Collect Information

### Discovery Questions for Client

#### Service/Product Inventory
1. What are all the services or products you currently offer?
2. How are they structured (individual services, packages, tiers)?
3. What's included in each service level or product tier?
4. Which services can be sold individually vs. only as part of packages?
5. Do you have any limited-time or seasonal offerings?

#### Pricing Structure
1. What are the price points for each service/product?
2. How do you handle pricing variations (company size, project scope, etc.)?
3. Do you offer payment plans or financing options?
4. What's your pricing strategy (premium, competitive, value-based)?
5. Which prices are fixed vs. custom quoted?

#### Customer Segmentation
1. Who is the ideal customer for each offering?
2. What company size/budget typically buys each tier?
3. Which offerings are best for beginners vs. advanced clients?
4. How do customer needs change as they grow?
5. What are the common upgrade paths clients follow?

#### Sales Strategy
1. Which service do most clients start with?
2. What are your most common upsells and cross-sells?
3. How do you bundle services for maximum value?
4. What objections do you face at each price point?
5. Which offerings have the highest profit margins?

## How to Use in Daily Operations

### Sales Content Creation
1. Reference offer architecture when writing service pages
2. Ensure all pricing and feature information is accurate and current
3. Use customer journey mapping to create logical content flows
4. Include appropriate upsell messaging in service descriptions

### Proposal Creation
1. Use templates and guidelines from offer architecture
2. Select appropriate services based on prospect qualification
3. Include relevant bundling options when beneficial
4. Apply correct pricing structure and payment terms

### Lead Qualification
1. Use service-specific qualification questions
2. Match prospect needs to appropriate service tier
3. Identify upsell/cross-sell opportunities early
4. Set proper expectations about pricing ranges

### Customer Success & Account Management
1. Monitor upgrade indicators from customer journey mapping
2. Proactively suggest appropriate service expansions
3. Use bundling strategies for renewals and expansions
4. Apply pricing psychology for retention conversations

```markdown
# Offer Architecture - Digital Marketing Consultancy

**Last Updated:** March 7, 2025
**Total Offerings:** 12

## Service Overview

### Core Service Categories
1. **Strategy & Consulting** - High-level planning and optimization
2. **Done-For-You Services** - Full implementation and management
3. **Training & Coaching** - Skill-building and guidance

### Entry Points by Budget
- **Budget-Conscious ($500-2K/month):** Coaching, audits, small projects
- **Standard Budget ($2K-8K/month):** Done-for-you services, retainers
- **Premium Budget ($8K+/month):** Full-service, enterprise consulting

---

## Service Architecture

### Strategy & Consulting

#### Marketing Strategy Audit
**Price:** $2,500 one-time
**Ideal For:** Businesses spending $5K+ on marketing with poor ROI
**Duration:** 2-3 weeks
**Team Size:** Senior strategist + analyst

**What's Included:**
- Complete marketing funnel analysis
- Competitive positioning review
- Channel performance evaluation
- 90-day optimization roadmap
- 2-hour strategy presentation

**What's NOT Included:**
- Implementation of recommendations
- Ongoing management
- Creative asset development

**Common Add-Ons:**
- Implementation planning: $1,500
- Team training session: $1,000

**Upsell Path:** Done-For-You Implementation ($4K-8K/month)
**Cross-Sell Opportunities:** Content strategy, team training

---

## Pricing Psychology

### Value Positioning
**Budget Tier:** "Get expert guidance that pays for itself"
**Standard Tier:** "Full-service solution with proven ROI"
**Premium Tier:** "Elite partnership for ambitious growth goals"

### Common Price Objections & Responses
1. **"That's too expensive"**
   - Response strategy: Break down cost per lead/customer acquired
   - Alternative offer: Audit + coaching instead of full management

2. **"I need to think about it"**
   - Response strategy: Offer audit first to prove value
   - Risk reversal: 30-day guarantee on retainer services

---

## Customer Journey Mapping

### Typical Progression Paths

#### Path 1: Audit → Done-For-You → Enterprise Partnership
**Timeline:** 3-6 months between upgrades
**Revenue Potential:** $75K+ annually
**Key Triggers:** Results from audit, team capacity constraints
```

## Success Criteria

1. Complete documentation of all current service offerings
2. Clear pricing structure with qualification guidelines
3. Logical customer journey paths mapped out
4. Bundling strategies that increase average deal size
5. Consistent pricing presentation across all sales materials
6. Reduced pricing objections through better positioning
7. Increased upsell/cross-sell revenue through systematic approach

This architecture ensures the AI Employee can accurately represent offerings, guide prospects to appropriate solutions, and maximize revenue through strategic positioning and progression paths.
