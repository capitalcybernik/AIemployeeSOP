# Complete AI Brain - Master Business Intelligence Builder

**Name:** Complete AI Brain  
**Description:** Master orchestrator skill that runs all brain-building skills sequentially to create a comprehensive business intelligence profile for the AI Employee.

## What This Skill Does

The Complete AI Brain skill acts as the central onboarding wizard for new clients. It systematically runs all other brain-building skills in the optimal sequence to create a complete business intelligence profile. This skill creates the master BRAIN.md file that links to all specialized intelligence files, giving the AI Employee a comprehensive understanding of the client's business, voice, expertise, and market position.

Think of this as the "first day on the job" process that transforms a blank AI Employee into one that knows the client's business inside and out.

## How to Collect Information

This skill orchestrates other skills, but here are the initial discovery questions to determine scope:

### Business Overview Questions
1. What is your primary business or profession?
2. How long have you been in business?
3. What are your main revenue streams?
4. Who is your target audience?
5. What sets you apart from competitors?

### Content Availability Assessment
1. Do you have existing marketing materials (website, brochures, presentations)?
2. Can you provide examples of your writing (emails, posts, articles)?
3. Do you have a professional bio or resume we can work from?
4. What social media platforms do you use for business?
5. Do you have any recorded interviews, podcasts, or videos?

### Goals and Priorities
1. What business goals is the AI Employee helping you achieve?
2. Which areas need the most support (content creation, lead generation, customer service)?
3. How formal or casual should the AI Employee's communication style be?
4. What topics should the AI focus on most?

```markdown
- Review quarterly for updates
- Update BRAIN.md links when adding new skills

```

## How to Use in Daily Operations

### Before Starting Any Task
1. Always reference BRAIN.md first to understand context
2. Check which specific intelligence files are relevant to the current task
3. Use appropriate voice, tone, and messaging based on the profiles

### Content Creation Workflow
1. Check VOICE-DNA.md for writing style
2. Reference BRAND-ARCHETYPE.md for messaging approach
3. Draw from STORIES.md for relevant anecdotes
4. Verify expertise level in EXPERTISE-MAP.md before writing about technical topics

### Client Communication
1. Use appropriate bio from BIOS.md based on context (formal email vs social media)
2. Reference MARKET-INTEL.md to understand audience and industry context
3. Apply voice patterns from VOICE-DNA.md consistently

### Maintenance Schedule
- **Weekly:** Review for any new information to incorporate
- **Monthly:** Update market intelligence and expertise levels
- **Quarterly:** Full brain review and optimization
