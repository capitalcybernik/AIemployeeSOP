# Competitor Analysis - Market Intelligence & Differentiation

**Name:** Competitor Analysis  
**Description:** Profiles top 5-10 competitors including their positioning, pricing, strengths, weaknesses, messaging comparison, and differentiation opportunities to inform content strategy and avoid copying competitor language.

## What This Skill Does

The Competitor Analysis skill creates comprehensive profiles of the client's main competitors, documenting their market positioning, pricing strategies, messaging approaches, strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for differentiation. This analysis helps the AI Employee create content that stands out from the competition, avoid copying competitor messaging, and identify unique positioning angles.

The analysis is updated quarterly to track competitor changes and market shifts, ensuring the client's content strategy remains differentiated and competitive.

## How to Collect Information

### Discovery Questions for Client

#### Competitor Identification
1. Who do you consider your top 5-10 direct competitors?
2. Who do prospects mention when they're comparison shopping?
3. Which companies consistently win deals you lose?
4. Who shows up in the same search results as your business?
5. Which competitors do clients mention when they leave you?
6. Are there any indirect competitors (different solutions to same problem)?

#### Competitive Landscape
1. How do competitors position themselves differently?
2. What are their main selling points or value propositions?
3. How do their prices compare to yours?
4. What do they do better than you?
5. What do you do better than them?
6. Which competitors are growing fastest in your market?

#### Market Positioning
1. How do competitors describe the problem they solve?
2. What language and terms do they use consistently?
3. Which benefits do they emphasize most?
4. How do they handle objections you also face?
5. What proof points do they use (case studies, statistics)?

#### Messaging Analysis
1. What tone and style do competitors use in their content?
2. Which channels do they focus on most?
3. How do they price and package their services?
4. What guarantees or risk reversals do they offer?
5. How do they differentiate from other competitors?

## How to Use in Daily Operations

### Content Creation
1. Before writing any marketing content, review competitor messaging to ensure differentiation
2. Use unique language and positioning identified in the analysis
3. Reference battle cards when creating comparison content
4. Incorporate market intelligence into thought leadership pieces

### Sales Support
1. Use battle cards during sales conversations when prospects mention competitors
2. Reference competitor weaknesses when highlighting your strengths
3. Prepare for common objections based on competitive landscape
4. Position pricing competitively using market intelligence

### Positioning Strategy
1. Regularly review positioning gaps for new opportunities
2. Monitor competitor changes that might affect your positioning
3. Update messaging to maintain differentiation
4. Identify underserved market segments

### Market Monitoring
1. Set up Google alerts for competitor names and key terms
2. Monitor competitor social media and content for changes
3. Track competitor pricing and service updates
4. Gather intelligence from prospects who mention competitors
