# Biography Versions - Multi-Context Bio Library

**Name:** Biography Versions  
**Description:** Creates multiple tailored versions of the business owner's biography for different contexts, platforms, and audiences to ensure the right bio is always available for any situation.

## What This Skill Does

The Biography Versions skill develops a comprehensive library of professionally crafted biographies ranging from one-sentence descriptions to full-length speaker introductions. Each version is optimized for specific contexts while maintaining consistent personal branding and key messaging.

This eliminates the common problem of having to write a bio from scratch for each opportunity and ensures consistent personal branding across all platforms and contexts. The AI Employee can instantly provide the appropriate bio length and style for any situation.

## How to Collect Information

### Background Information Gathering

#### Professional Details
1. What is your current job title and company?
2. How many years of experience do you have in your field?
3. What are your primary areas of expertise?
4. What is your educational background (degrees, certifications, notable institutions)?
5. What are your most significant professional achievements?

#### Career Highlights
1. What awards, recognitions, or honors have you received?
2. Have you been featured in media or publications?
3. What notable companies have you worked with?
4. Have you spoken at conferences or events?
5. What results or outcomes are you most proud of?

#### Personal Elements
1. Where are you based (city/region)?
2. What personal interests relate to your professional brand?
3. Do you have any unique background elements (military service, interesting career path)?
4. What personal mission or passion drives your work?
5. Any family elements you like to include (if appropriate for professional bios)?

#### Platform-Specific Needs
1. What social media platforms do you use professionally?
2. Do you speak at conferences or events?
3. Do you need bios for media interviews or press releases?
4. Are there specific industries where you need specialized positioning?
5. What length requirements do you commonly encounter?

### Bio Style Preferences
1. Do you prefer third-person or first-person bios?
2. How formal vs conversational should your bio sound?
3. Are there specific achievements you want emphasized?
4. Are there aspects of your background you prefer to minimize?
5. What tone best represents your personal brand?

## Platform-Specific Versions

### LinkedIn About Section
**Style:** First-person, conversational but professional  
**Length:** 125-150 words  
**Focus:** Value to connections, experience, personal approach

### Twitter Bio
**Style:** Third-person, concise, keyword-rich  
**Length:** Under 160 characters  
**Focus:** What you do + who you help + location/contact

### Instagram Bio
**Style:** First-person, personality-driven  
**Length:** Under 150 characters  
**Focus:** Personal brand + what you're about + call to action

### Speaker Bio Template
**Style:** Third-person, achievement-focused  
**Length:** 75-150 words depending on event  
**Focus:** Credibility, expertise, audience relevance

### Media Bio Template  
**Style:** Third-person, newsworthy elements  
**Length:** 100-200 words  
**Focus:** Current role, major achievements, quotable elements

### Podcast Guest Bio
**Style:** Third-person, conversational  
**Length:** 50-100 words  
**Focus:** Expertise relevant to show topic, personality elements

## How to Use in Daily Operations

### Bio Selection Process
1. Identify the context and platform requirements
2. Check length restrictions and style expectations
3. Select the appropriate version from the library
4. Customize if needed for specific audience or topic
5. Use as-is or adapt language for the specific opportunity

### Content Creation Integration
1. Use bio elements for author bylines on content
2. Reference achievements when establishing credibility
3. Pull quotes or key phrases for social media
4. Use consistent positioning across all content

### Media and Speaking Preparation
1. Provide appropriate bio length for event organizers
2. Ensure bio matches speaking topic and audience
3. Include relevant achievements for credibility
4. Prepare multiple options for different introduction styles

### Personal Branding Consistency
1. Ensure all bio versions tell the same core story
2. Use consistent language and positioning across platforms
3. Update all versions when major changes occur
4. Monitor how others introduce you and adjust as needed
