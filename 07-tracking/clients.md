## Status Definitions

### Client Status
- **Discovery**: Initial call scheduled or completed
- **Building**: AI employee under construction
- **Testing**: Client testing in private environment
- **Live**: Deployed and active
- **Optimizing** : Live and in optimization cycle
- **Churned** : Client cancelled

### Pipeline Status
- **Lead** : Expressed interest
- **Contacted** : Outreach sent
- **Call Scheduled** : Discovery call booked
- **Proposal Sent** : Tier and scope discussed
- **Closed Won** : Signed up
- **Closed Lost** : Passed or went silent
