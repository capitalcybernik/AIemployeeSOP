# Skills Catalog

## Tier 1: Standard (pick up to 5)
## Tier 2: Pro (unlimited)

---

## Marketing Skills

### 📍 Location Page Builder
**What it does:** Creates SEO optimized landing pages for every city and service area the business covers.
**Setup:** Provide list of target cities, service descriptions, and business details. AI generates unique pages with local relevance.
**Platforms:** Website (pages pushed via CMS or static site)
**Tier:** Both

### 📝 Blog & Content Creation
**What it does:** Writes keyword rich, SEO optimized blog posts on topics relevant to the business and industry.
**Setup:** Define topic categories, target keywords, posting frequency, and brand voice guidelines.
**Platforms:** Website CMS (WordPress, etc.)
**Tier:** Both

### 🔍 SEO Optimization
**What it does:** Handles on page SEO including meta tags, schema markup, keyword strategy, and internal linking.
**Setup:** Provide website access, target keywords, and competitor URLs for analysis.
**Platforms:** Website
**Tier:** Both

### 📧 Email Marketing
**What it does:** Creates and manages newsletters, promotional campaigns, and drip sequences.
**Setup:** Connect email platform (Mailchimp, GHL, etc.), define audience segments, set frequency.
**Platforms:** Email
**Tier:** Both

### 📱 Social Media Management
**What it does:** Creates and schedules posts across social platforms. Maintains consistent posting schedule.
**Setup:** Connect social accounts, define content themes, set posting schedule and tone.
**Platforms:** LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, X
**Tier:** Both

### 🏗️ Service Page Builder
**What it does:** Creates dedicated, optimized pages for each service the business offers.
**Setup:** List all services with descriptions, pricing info (if public), and differentiators.
**Platforms:** Website
**Tier:** Both

## Customer Engagement Skills

### ⭐ Review Request Follow Ups
**What it does:** Automatically requests reviews from customers after completed jobs/services.
**Setup:** Define trigger (job completion, X days after service), review platform links (Google, Yelp), message templates.
**Platforms:** SMS, Email
**Tier:** Both

### 💬 Website Live Chat
**What it does:** Provides instant answers to website visitors 24/7. Captures leads, answers FAQs, and escalates when needed.
**Setup:** Install chat widget on website, configure FAQ responses, set escalation triggers.
**Platforms:** Website
**Tier:** Both

### 🧾 Estimate Follow Ups
**What it does:** Automatically follows up with customers who received estimates but haven't responded.
**Setup:** Define follow up timing (24h, 48h, 1 week), message templates, and give up threshold.
**Platforms:** SMS, Email
**Tier:** Both

---

## Operations Skills

### 📊 Daily Business Reports
**What it does:** Generates morning briefings on tasks, metrics, and items needing attention.
**Setup:** Define what metrics to track, report timing, and delivery method.
**Platforms:** Email, Discord, SMS
**Tier:** Both

---

## Advanced Skills (Pro Tier Recommended)

### 🔗 CRM Integration
**What it does:** Syncs conversations, leads, and customer data with the business CRM.
**Setup:** API connection to CRM (Tekmetric, Steer, GHL, ShopWare, etc.)
**Platforms:** CRM
**Tier:** Pro recommended

### 📅 Booking Integration
**What it does:** Allows customers to book appointments directly through AI interactions.
**Setup:** Connect booking system, define available slots, confirmation flow.
**Platforms:** Website chat, SMS
**Tier:** Pro recommended

### 📈 Analytics & Reporting
**What it does:** Comprehensive performance tracking across all platforms and skills.
**Setup:** Define KPIs, reporting frequency, and delivery method.
**Platforms:** Email, Dashboard
**Tier:** Pro recommended

---

## Skill Selection Guide

| Business Need | Recommended Skills | Min Tier |
|---|---|---|
| "We need to show up on Google" | Location Pages, SEO, Blog Content | Standard |
| "We need more reviews" | Review Follow Ups, Live Chat | Standard |
| "We need a full marketing team" | All marketing skills + Social + Email | Pro |
| "We lose estimates all the time" | Estimate Follow Ups, Live Chat | Standard |
| "We want everything connected" | All skills + CRM + Booking + Analytics | Pro |
