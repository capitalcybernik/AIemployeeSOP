# Discovery Call Questionnaire

Send to client before the call, or use as a guide during the call.

---

## About Your Business

1. Business name:
2. Industry/type:
3. Website URL:
4. How many locations?
5. Service area (cities/counties):
6. How many employees?
7. Who handles your marketing today?

## Your Customers

8. Who is your ideal customer?
9. How do customers typically find you? (Google, referrals, social media, etc.)
10. What are the top 5 questions customers ask?
11. What are customers' biggest complaints or frustrations?

## Current Online Presence

12. How many Google reviews do you have? Average rating?
13. Are you active on social media? Which platforms?
14. Do you have a blog or publish content regularly?
15. Do you run email campaigns or newsletters?

## Pain Points

16. What takes up most of your time that you wish you could delegate?
17. What marketing tasks are you NOT doing that you know you should?
18. Have you tried any AI tools or chatbots before? How did it go?

## AI Employee Goals

19. If you had an AI employee starting tomorrow, what would you want it to do first?
20. What should your AI employee NEVER do? (important for safety)
21. How should your AI employee sound? (professional, casual, friendly, authoritative)
22. Who should it escalate to when it can't help? (name + contact method)

## Technical

23. What tools/software do you currently use? (CRM, booking system, email platform, etc.)
24. Do you have access to your website backend? (WordPress, Squarespace, etc.)
25. Would you want the AI on your website, SMS, email, or all of the above?

## Budget and Timeline

26. Which tier interests you? ($500/month standard or $1,000/month pro)
27. When would you like to have your AI employee live?
28. Any upcoming events, promotions, or deadlines we should know about?
